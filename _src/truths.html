At STARLINE, we are committed to transparency, truth, and our customers.

We have compiled a list of truths about STARLINE Industries to inform your choices.

1. STARLINE Industries is committed to a clean, safe supply chain.

We ensure that throughout our supply chain, we follow all local* labour laws and safety practices.

By following these laws and regulations, we are confident that our supply chain is safe and healthy for all workers involved.

2. We are committed to privacy and safety.

At STARLINE, we don't collect any personally identifiable information from any of our services*. All information we collect is anonymized.

Please see our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

3. We follow the regulations of the governments we operate under.

We ensure that we are compliant to any and all laws in the governments under which we operate.

Recently, the S7RC and S7SAZ's vast government overreach has made this more difficult in Sector 7, but we continue to comply to their authoritarian rules as STARLINE SECTOR 7*.

The S7RC's External Corporations and Fair Labour Practices Act and External Corporations and Citizen's Privacy Act continue to expand their reach into STARLINE's external operations. We encourage all customers in Sector 7 to leave the sector to continue receiving the best services from STARLINE Industries.

4. We are dedicated to waste reduction.

STARLINE Industries has dedicated over 13 million Credits to waste reduction efforts intergalactically.

5. Our employees are thriving.

In an internal survey taken in the 97th Cycle our findings show that 90% of workers* are fully satisfied with their jobs at STARLINE.

*1: Laws local to the Outer Union, where we are based.

*2: "Services" meaning any service by STARLINE, excluding any and all of its subsidaries.

*3: STARLINE SECTOR 7 is forbidden from operating in the S7SAZ.

*4: Survey taken of roles Upper Management and higher.
